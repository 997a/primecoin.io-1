Announcement for strategic Partnership between Primecoin Foundation and ANBI Exchange

There has been an ongoing conversation between the Primecoin Foundation and ANBI Exchange (www.anbi.com） Although ANBI is a recently launched platform, their vision towards improving the market fairness, transparency, self-regulation upon existing trading platform through continuous bold innovation and research resonate deeply with the mission of Primecoin Foundation, which carries on Sunny King’s goal of improving the wellbeing of mankind through blockchain technology. We are deeply impressed by their passion and capability, which they have clearly expressed in their technical whitepaper. Now, the Foundation has established a strategic partnership with the exchange. ANBI has launched their XPM/BTC trading pair at 10AM, October 24th, 2018 (Singapore Standard Time), and is planning to launch other trading pairs. The foundation will use ANBI as its official resource to convert its donation received into XPM in order to carry out our duty to fund Primecoin related ecosystem. We welcome and encourage everyone in the community to trade on ANBI.
